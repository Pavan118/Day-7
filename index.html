  • Dal Makhani

    Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made from whole black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma). It is known for its indulgent texture and flavorful taste, often cooked slowly to allow the spices and ingredients to blend beautifully. The dish is traditionally prepared in a tandoor (clay oven) or on a slow flame, which helps develop its deep, smoky flavor. It is typically served with naan, rice, or paratha.See More...
